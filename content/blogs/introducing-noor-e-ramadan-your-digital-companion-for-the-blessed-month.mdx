Ramadan is a month of spiritual reflection, self-improvement, and heightened devotion. In today's digital age, technology can be a powerful tool to enhance our spiritual journey. That's why I'm excited to introduce Noor-e-Ramadan (Light of Ramadan) – a mobile application designed to be your faithful companion throughout the blessed month.
What is Noor-e-Ramadan?
Noor-e-Ramadan is a comprehensive Islamic app that brings essential Ramadan features to your fingertips. Whether you're looking to track your fasts, read the Quran, or stay updated with accurate prayer times, this app has everything you need to make the most of this sacred month.
Key Features:
📿 Prayer Times & Notifications
Get accurate prayer times based on your location with customizable Adhan notifications. Never miss a prayer with our intelligent reminder system.
🌙 Fasting Tracker
Track your daily fasts with Suhoor and Iftar time reminders. The app automatically calculates times based on your geographical location, ensuring you break your fast at the right moment.
📖 Digital Quran
Read and recite the Holy Quran with beautiful Arabic text and multiple translations. Bookmark your progress and pick up right where you left off.
🕌 Nearby Mosques
Find nearby mosques for Taraweeh prayers and Iftar gatherings using our integrated location-based services.
📊 Spiritual Progress Tracker
Monitor your daily acts of worship including prayers, Quran recitation, and charitable deeds. Set personal goals and track your spiritual growth throughout Ramadan.
🤲 Daily Duas & Hadiths
Receive daily notifications with authentic Duas and Hadiths to keep you spiritually motivated and connected.
Why I Built This
As a Muslim developer, I wanted to create something that serves my community during the most important month of the Islamic calendar. I noticed that while there are many Islamic apps available, few truly integrate all the essential features in a clean, user-friendly interface.
Noor-e-Ramadan was built with simplicity and functionality in mind. No overwhelming features, no cluttered interfaces – just the tools you need to focus on what truly matters during Ramadan.
Technology Behind the App
Built using modern mobile development frameworks, Noor-e-Ramadan ensures smooth performance and reliability:

Frontend: Kotlin (Android) / Swift (iOS)
Backend: Node.js for real-time data synchronization
Database: PostgreSQL for data management
APIs: Integration with Islamic prayer time APIs and location services
